Fig. 4 ( a ) Arteriogram of a patient with bleeding from a duodenal ulcer after celiac injection. There was continued bleeding in spite of endoscopic clipping and injection of epinephrine into ulcer bed. The arrow indicates gastroduodenal artery with no active extravasation. The clip noticed on fluoroscopy is in the third/fourth portion of duodenum. ( b ) Arrows indicate gastroduodenal artery coil embolized using multiple coils. The vessel is occluded just beyond its origin from the proper hepatic artery
