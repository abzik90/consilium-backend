heading: "Bleeding hemorrhoids"
---

The Association of Coloproctology of Great Britain and Ireland conducted a national audit in 2005 which included 695 patients undergoing stapled hemorrhoidopexy for bleeding or prolapsed hemorrhoids. This audit concluded that it is a safe and effective procedure with good short-term outcomes and requires longer-term follow-up [26].
