heading: "Treatment"
---

Early endoscopy is critical in the diagnosis and treatment of patients with Mallory-Weiss tears [12]. If, on endoscopy, active bleeding is not observed, no endoscopic therapy is indicated and the patient should be observed for re-bleeding for 48 h. If bleeding is observed, endoscopic therapy (such as injections, electrocautery, or clipping) is the first-line treatment of patients with on-going bleeding
