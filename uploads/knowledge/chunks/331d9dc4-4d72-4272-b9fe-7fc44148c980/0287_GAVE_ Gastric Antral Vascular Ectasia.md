heading: "GAVE: Gastric Antral Vascular Ectasia"
---

Management of GAVE involves [1] treating symptoms of acute or chronic bleeding and [2] preventing future bleeding. For symptomatic blood loss from GAVE, initial management includes fluid resuscitation and transfusion for acute episodes, along with iron supplementation for chronic anemia. To stop active bleeding and prevent future episodes, endoscopic ablation is the first-line therapy as well as treating underlying medical co-morbidities.
