An esophagogastroduodenoscopy (EGD) has a much higher sensitivity and the added potential for therapy and thus some consider NG lavage unnecessary in the diagnostic role. On the other hand, there is agreement that NG lavage is useful in clearing out the stomach of blood and clot, thus making the subsequent EGD easier to perform. The American College of Physicians consensus recommended considering NG lavage for use as an adjunct to endoscopy [4].
