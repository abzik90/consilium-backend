heading: "Prevention of Recurrence"
---

Both oral and intravenous infusions of PPI have been shown to reduce recurrent bleeding, decrease hospital stay, and reduce the need for blood transfusion. However, the same effect has not been demonstrated with the use of H2 receptor antagonists, and neither has shown any effect on mortality [29, 43, 47].
