Stapled hemorrhoidopexy, also known as the procedure for prolapsing hemorrhoids (PPH), has been gaining popularity since its introduction in the late 1990s. Longo first described the technique in 1998 whereby a specially designed circumferential stapler is carefully applied via a proctoscope [25] and used to secure the anal cushions back in their anatomic position (Fig. 6).
