heading: "Ulcerative Colitis"
---

Emergency colectomy is indicated for massive hemorrhage, fulminant toxic colitis, or perforation. Subtotal colectomy with ileostomy and rectosigmoid closure is usually the procedure of choice because most critically ill patients cannot tolerate total proctocolectomy. The rectosigmoid stump may be removed at another setting since it may represent a site for disease reactivation.
