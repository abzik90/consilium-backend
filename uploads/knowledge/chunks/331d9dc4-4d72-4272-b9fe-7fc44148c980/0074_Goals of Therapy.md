heading: "Goals of Therapy"
---

Prevention of the morbidity and mortality associated with UGIB should involve assessment and therapy aimed at three approaches: hemodynamic resuscitation, cessation of bleeding source, and prevention of future recurrence. Although each aim is independent, there is inherent overlap and each should proceed in a concurrent manner to achieve the best outcomes.
