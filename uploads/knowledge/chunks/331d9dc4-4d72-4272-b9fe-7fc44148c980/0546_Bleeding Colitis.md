heading: "Bleeding Colitis"
---

Classification of colitis (Table 1) depends on the etiology of the offending factor, but in general, the clinical presentation and endoscopic appearance are similar in different types of colitis. Endoscopic examination usually reveals a friable mucosa with edema, erythema, and tendency to bleed upon manipulation.
