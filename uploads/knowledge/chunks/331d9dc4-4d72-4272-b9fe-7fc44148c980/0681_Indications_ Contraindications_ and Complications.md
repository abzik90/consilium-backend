heading: "Indications, Contraindications, and Complications"
---

Capsule retention is the most significant complication associated with this procedure, however, fortunately it is rare, occurring in less than 1% of cases [9]. The patients at greatest risk are those with Crohn's disease (5-13%), radiation enteritis, chronic NSAID use, tumors, post-surgical anastomotic stenosis, clinically apparent adhesions, and those with severe motility disorders [10, 11] (Fig. 3).
