PTC is a relatively safe procedure with a less than 1% rate of hemobilia formation. Hemobilia following TIPS occurs less than 5% of the time. In contrast, PBD placement is associated with an 8% hemobilia rate [12]. Hemobilia may result from therapeutic hepatobiliary procedures as well. In our own experience, we have seen a case of bleeding into the gallbladder after a laparoscopic radiofrequency ablation of a liver mass (Fig. 2).
