heading: "Esophagogastroduodenoscopy in Non-variceal Bleeding"
---

In spite of the excellent results with EGD, the procedure is not without complications. It can cause gastrointestinal perforations, precipitation of gastrointestinal bleeding, aspiration pneumonia, respiratory arrest, and cardiovascular complications [11]. The incidence of complications is low, but it is important to be certain that in each patient the benefit of the procedure outweighs the risk.
