heading: "Prevention of Initial Bleed"
---

Due to the high mortality associated with initial bleed, much interest exists in developing methods of prophylaxis. Operative prophylaxis, while initially thought to be effective, has been abandoned over the past two decades secondary to prohibitive mortality rates [1, 2].
