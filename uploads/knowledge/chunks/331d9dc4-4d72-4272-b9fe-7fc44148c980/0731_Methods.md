This regimen is then repeated for the second most suspicious distribution. Angiography is acquired with the injection rates of 5-7 mL/s into either the celiac or superior mesenteric arterial distribution for a total injection of 20 mL. In the inferior mesenteric arterial distribution, 3 mL/s or hand injection for a total of 10 mL is injected. This allows for adequate opacification of the distal vascular bed where the bleeding originates (Fig. 2a, b, c and d).
