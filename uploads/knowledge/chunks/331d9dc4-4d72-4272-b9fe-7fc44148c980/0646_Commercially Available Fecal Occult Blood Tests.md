heading: "Commercially Available Fecal Occult Blood Tests"
---

There are three main categories of fecal occult blood testing methods commercially available: guaiac based, heme-porphyrin based, and immunochemical based. Depending on the particular testing method used, occult blood loss can be detected from any location in the gastrointestinal tract.
