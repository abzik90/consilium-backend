heading: "Hemodynamic Resuscitation"
---

During resuscitation efforts, all patients should remain nil per os . This will not only aid endoscopic measures, but also help protect the patient's airway. If there is any concern for the protection of the patient's airway, either because of potential aspiration or due to mental status, then the patient should be promptly intubated endotracheally.
