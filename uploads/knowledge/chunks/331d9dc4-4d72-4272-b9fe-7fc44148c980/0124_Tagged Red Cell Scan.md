heading: "Tagged Red Cell Scan"
---

Technetium 99m-labeled red blood cell scan, also known as tagged red cell scan, can also be used in patients with obscure UGIB. Red blood cells are labeled with technetium 99 and injected into the celiac artery in order to detect upper GI bleeding. This nuclear medicine scan allows for the detection of bleeds that are much slower, with a rate greater than 0.1-0.4 mL/min.
