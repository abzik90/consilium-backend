heading: "Management of Acute Bleeding"
---

Medical management, alone, fails in up to 20% of cases. In these cases, there are a variety of interventional procedures with documented efficacy in the treatment of variceal bleeding. Foremost amongst these is endoscopic management of variceal bleeding, which includes sclerotherapy and banding.
