The stigmata of bleeding on endoscopy such as active bleeding or adherent clots is used to identified the bleeding source in upper endoscopies (Fig. 3a); however, most endoscopists do not perform urgent colonoscopy to identify stigmata of bleeding from lower gastrointestinal lesions, but rather perform elective colonoscopy when the patient has had a chance to undergo a colonic purge that would facilitate visualization.
