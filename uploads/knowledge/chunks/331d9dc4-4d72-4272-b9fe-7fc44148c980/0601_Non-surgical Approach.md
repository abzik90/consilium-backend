heading: "Non-surgical Approach"
---

The first step in the management of patients with lower GI bleeding from A VM is to correct any coagulopathic condition. Aggressive volume resuscitation is sometimes required. In general, a conservative approach to patients who are hemodynamically stable is recommended because most bleeding AVM will cease spontaneously. Treatment is not advocated in asymptomatic patients with incidental AVM finings on routine colonoscopy.
