A 12 h fast is all that is required to prepare patients for antegrade DBE, and the standard colonoscopy bowel cleansing protocol should be followed for retrograde DBE. Despite improvements in procedure time with increasing experience, these remain relatively prolonged procedures as shown in one European study where the mean procedural time after 10 attempts reduced from 109 ± 44 min to 92 ± 37 min [26]; subsequently it may be more appropriate to use general anesthesia as distinct from conscious sedation although this is not an absolute requirement.
