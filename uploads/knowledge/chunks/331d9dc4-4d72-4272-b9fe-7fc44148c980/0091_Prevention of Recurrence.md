heading: "Prevention of Recurrence"
---

Patients with cirrhosis have increased infection rates within the community that are even more greatly elevated when admitted to a hospital. Variceal bleeding disrupts normal mucosal integrity, thus allowing higher rates of bacterial infection. Prophylactic antibiotics in cirrhotic patients hospitalized for UGIB have been demonstrated to reduce overall infections complications, recurrent bleeding, and mortality [48, 49].
