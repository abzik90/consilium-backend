Adenoma. Small bowel adenomas represent approximately 25% benign small bowel tumors. Similar to adenomas found in the large intestines, they may occasionally cause bleeding. The duodenum, especially the periampullary region, is the most frequent location for adenomas. However, they may occasionally be found throughout the small intestines [6].
