The objective of partial shunts is similar to that of selective shunts. They incompletely decompress the entire portal venous system while preserving some hepatic portal flow. Originally described by Sarfeh, it utilizes a small diameter PTFE graft for shunting. However, due to its use of synthetic material, there is a higher incidence of thrombosis and, therefore, it is less favored than the selective Warren shunt.
