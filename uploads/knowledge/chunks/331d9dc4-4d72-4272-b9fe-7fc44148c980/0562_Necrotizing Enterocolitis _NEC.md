heading: "Necrotizing Enterocolitis (NEC)"
---

Necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC) is common cause of colitis in newborns that affects premature patients. NEC presents with gas accumulation in the bowel wall and progresses to necrosis that can lead to perforation, peritonitis, and sepsis.
