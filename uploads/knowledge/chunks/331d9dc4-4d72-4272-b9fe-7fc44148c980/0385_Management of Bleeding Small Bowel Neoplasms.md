Intraoperative enteroscopy may be beneficial for localizing small bowel lesions. As described above, the location of the identified lesion is immediately apparent to the surgeon by transillumination and physical palpation. When combined with intraoperative endoscopy, surgery is able to correctly localize 50-100% of bleeding small bowel lesions [37-39].
