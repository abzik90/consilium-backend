Small bowel follow-through (SBFT). Similar to plain films, SBFT is relatively inexpensive and noninvasive. Contrast tracking through the small bowel may demonstrate the presence of strictures, large masses, large polyps, tumors, and deep ulcerations; however, smaller ulcerations and tumors are more difficult to identify
