heading: "Urgent Arteriography"
---

A second appropriate use of arteriography is in patients who experience LGIB, have a colonoscopy that localizes the area of hemorrhage, but rebleed despite endoscopic treatment. A second colonoscopy with repeat endoscopic treatment may be useful, but it is potentially more effective to employ a second treatment modality such as arteriography before proceeding to surgical intervention for these difficult cases [12].
