heading: "Bleeding Colitis"
---

By definition, colitis refers to inflammation of the mucosa and can be associated with inflammation of other segments of the GI tract (e.g., ileitis) or be limited to the colon. Proctitis refers to inflammation limited to the rectal mucosa.
