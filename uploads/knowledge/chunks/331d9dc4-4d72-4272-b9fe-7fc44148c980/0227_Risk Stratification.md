Table 5 Forrest classification

Grade I: active pulsatile bleeding, Rebleeding risk (%) = 70-90. Grade Ib: active nonpulsatile bleeding, Rebleeding risk (%) = 10-20. Grade IIa: nonbleeding visible vessel, Rebleeding risk (%) = 40-50. Grade IIb: adherent clot, Rebleeding risk (%) = 10-20. Grade IIc: pigmented spot, Rebleeding risk (%) = 1-2. Grade III: no signs of recent bleeding, Rebleeding risk (%) = 1-2
