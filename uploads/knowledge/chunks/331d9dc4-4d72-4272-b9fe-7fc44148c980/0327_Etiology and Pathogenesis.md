Alcoholism and hiatal hernias are the two main risk factors identified for the development of Mallory-Weiss tears. Heavy alcohol use associated with emesis has been noted in up to 80% of Mallory-Weiss syndrome [8-10]. Similarly, hiatal hernia is found in almost all patients with bleeding.
