heading: "Ulcerative Colitis"
---

Bleeding from mild disease confined to the rectum and sigmoid colon can be treated topically, thus systemic treatment is not always necessary. Hydrocortisone or 5-ASA enemas can be quite effective in the treatment of left-sided disease.
